Creator Command

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Creator Command ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Creator Command application available at creatorcommandrepository.vercel.app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of connected platforms:

  • Account information: your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth)
  • Connected platform credentials: OAuth access tokens for platforms you choose to connect (Instagram, Threads, YouTube, TikTok). We store tokens securely and never expose them to other users.
  • Platform profile data: your handle, follower count, and post performance metrics pulled from connected platforms
  • Scheduled content: posts, captions, media URLs, and scheduled publish times that you create within the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve Creator Command:

  • Authenticate your account and keep your session secure
  • Connect to your social media platforms on your behalf
  • Display your analytics, post history, and upcoming schedule
  • Automatically publish posts at times you have scheduled
  • Generate personalized tips and insights based on your content performance

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for their own purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Creator Command integrates with the following third-party services to function. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Meta (Instagram & Threads): facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • Google (YouTube): policies.google.com/privacy
  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Supabase (database & authentication): supabase.com/privacy

We only request the minimum permissions needed from each platform. You can revoke access at any time from your platform's app settings or from the Settings page in Creator Command.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a Supabase database with row-level security enabled, meaning each user can only access their own records. Access tokens are stored encrypted and are never logged or exposed in API responses. We use HTTPS for all data in transit.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data (including connected platform tokens, scheduled posts, and analytics history) is permanently removed from our database within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect any connected platform at any time via Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Creator Command is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Creator Command after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
